Welcome to the Monopolist Boycott Game

  • You are either a monopolist or a consumer.
  • In each round, the monopolist sets a price.
  • Consumers will decide whether to buy at that price.
  • Your earnings depend on your role, the price, and the decisions made.
  • If you are the monopolist: your earnings = price × number of consumers who buy.
  • If you are a consumer: if you buy, your earnings = your value − price; if you don’t buy, your earnings = 0.
