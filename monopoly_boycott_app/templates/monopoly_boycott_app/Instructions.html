Welcome to the Monopolist Boycott Game

This experiment will consist of 20 rounds.

  • You are either a monopolist or a consumer.
  • In each round, the monopolist sets a price.
  • Consumers will decide whether to buy at that price.
  • In some rounds, consumers can communicate using a chat box.
  • In all rounds, your earnings depend on your role, the price, and the buying decision.
